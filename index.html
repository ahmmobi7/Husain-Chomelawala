Biography

A developer who solves real problems

Husain Chomelawala is a seasoned IT professional with over 15 years of hands-on experience across a wide range of technologies, industries, and organizational contexts. His career has spanned multiple roles — from development and architecture to project management and consulting — giving him a uniquely holistic perspective on building software that actually works for real users.

Having worked on several multinational projects across different sectors, Husain understands the intersection of technical excellence and practical usability. He has collaborated with cross-functional teams across geographies, delivered systems under demanding enterprise requirements, and consistently brought disciplined engineering practices to every project he has been part of.

Cleanssup was born from a simple personal frustration — watching a family member's phone slow to a crawl because WhatsApp had silently consumed all available storage. What started as a weekend project grew into a carefully crafted tool built on modern Android architecture, with a privacy-first philosophy at its core.

Every design decision in Cleanssup was made with user privacy as the primary constraint. No servers. No uploads. No accounts. Your data stays on your device.
